Food Ordering and Collection

Heretaunga College offers a tasty and convenient lunch ordering system throughout the week, giving students a variety of options to suit different tastes. Each day features a different provider, and all orders are picked up from the Cafe during the lunch break. Students are reminded to place their orders by 9:00 am day of delivery and to collect them promptly at lunchtime. These offerings not only make lunchtime easier but also add a bit of excitement to the school week.

Monday: Sushi Planet
Tuesday: Domino's Pizza
Thursday: Tina's Dumplings
Friday: Pita Pit
Visit Lunch Online for the full menu.
