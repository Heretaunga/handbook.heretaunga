Lunch Time Activities

At Heretaunga College, lunchtime is always buzzing with energy thanks to the wide variety of activities on offer throughout the year. Students can join in on regular weekly events such as Monday Hangs in the Kickstart Pavilion, where friends gather to relax and catch up, or test their skills in Tuesday Basketball on the courts. Wednesdays bring extra excitement with inflatables set up on the field, creating a fun and lively atmosphere for all. Thursdays are perfect for competitive spirits with Bucketball on the courts, while Fridays wrap up the week with a game of Volleyball out on the field, encouraging teamwork and friendly rivalry.

Special themed weeks and events add even more variety to lunchtimes, giving students the chance to try new things and celebrate together. During Math Week, students can enjoy fun games and challenges in the Spine every morning, with exciting prizes to be won and plenty of opportunities to team up with friends. Cultural celebrations like Multicultural Be Yourself Day bring the school community together, with vibrant food stalls outside the hall showcasing flavours from around the world. These activities not only make lunchtimes more engaging but also help foster school spirit, inclusiveness, and a sense of belonging for everyone.
