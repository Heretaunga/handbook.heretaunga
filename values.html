PRIDE Values

At Heretaunga College we have our Pride Values. In 2012 we became a Positive Behaviour for Learning (PB4L) school. This meant that as a school we came up with a set of values that would shape how we behaved in the classroom, on campus and in our community. The PB4L Team revisited these values in 2020 to make sure they are still relevant to our school today.

Our PRIDE values are as follows:

Participation - Whai wāhitanga
Respect - Manaakitanga
Integrity - Ngākau Pono
Determination - Te Hiringa
Empathy - Aroha
