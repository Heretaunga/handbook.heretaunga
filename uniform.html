Uniform

The Heretaunga College uniform encompasses both the uniform items and the expectations around appearance. Students wearing the Heretaunga College uniform are expected to wear uniform items correctly and follow appearance expectations in representing our college and our PRIDE values. Upon enrolment, students and caregivers have signed a declaration acknowledging agreement to follow the Heretaunga College uniform expectations.

Branded Uniform Items are only available through NZ Uniforms
The following items are branded specifically for Heretaunga College:

  1. School jersey (new merino non-scratch blend)
  2. School cardigan (new merino non-scratch blend)
  3. School vest - seniors only (new merino non-scratch blend)
  4. School skirt, trousers, shorts
  5. School sweatshirt
  6. Senior school shirt (long or short sleeve, fitted or unfitted) and school tie
  7. Junior school grey shirt
  8. Junior school polo shirt
  9. Senior school blazers (cannot wear a school hoodie underneath)
  10. Junior PE uniform
  11. Senior PE uniform
  12. School scarf
  13. School cap or bucket hat
  14. School branded black jacket
  15. School hoodie (discontinued, but may still be worn by students)

Other Non-Branded Uniform Items

  1. Plain black jackets either sleeveless or with sleeves - no cotton / sweatshirt / fleece materials, no other colours permitted. No logos permitted.
  2. Plain black shoes - including trainers. Shoes must have a plain black sole and no other colours. No high-top shoes, boots or high heels permitted. Preferably leather for health and safety in the classroom. No coloured logos.
  3. Plain black stockings / tights (not to be worn with socks)
  4. Plain black formal lavalava, also known as sulu or tupenu or ie-faitaga (no added colours, designs or logos)
  5. Sandals - black Roman sandals or black Birkenstock sandals with backstrap only (see photos). Sandals must have a strapped back. No socks to be worn with sandals.
  6. Socks - plain black or white socks only. Must be below the knee. No other colours, no logos (HC woollen school socks may also be worn)
  7. Cap or bucket hat - plain black, no other colours, no logos.


Appearance Expectations:

  1. Hair
    Natural hair colour shades only (blonde, black, auburn, brown). If hair is dyed, ONE natural colour is permitted.

  2. Nails
    Rounded nails. Clear or natural colour polish is permitted. No other colours. No nail extensions permitted for health and safety reasons.

  3. Jewellery
    ONE bracelet. ONE necklace. ONE ring on ONE finger. Items must be removed if required for health and safety reasons in specific curriculum courses.

  4. Piercings
    Ears: Studs and sleeper earrings (8mm in diameter) are permitted. No hoops, bars, chains, dangling earrings (for health and safety reasons).
    Nose: ONE small stud (2mm) OR ONE nose ring (8mm in diameter) permitted. No septum piercings. No other facial piercings are permitted.

  5. Tattoos
    No gang affiliation or offensive tattoos permitted. Upper Hutt Industry Appearance Bylaw 2022 clause 7.3: "No Operator shall allow or carry out any Prescribed Practice on any person under the age of 16 years without the written permission of that person's parent or legal guardian."

  6. Facial Hair
    Facial hair is allowed.
