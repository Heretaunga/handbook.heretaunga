Heretaunga HC-STUDENT Connection Guide

Chromebook Steps

For Chromebooks you will need to open the Wi-Fi menu at the bottom right of your screen and click HC-STUDENT.

This will open a new window with the title 'Join Wi-Fi network'.

Ensure that your EAP method is set to PEAP.

Ensure that your EAP Phase 2 Authentication is set to Automatic.

Ensure that your Server CA certificate is set to 'Do not check'.

In the box labelled Identity, enter your full school email address

(firstname.lastname@heretaunga.school.nz)

And in the boxed labelled password, enter the password for your school email address.

You are now connected to HC-STUDENT

Laptop Steps

For non-Chromebook laptops and other personal devices, start by opening the Wi-Fi menu at the bottom right-hand corner of your screen and clicking on HC-STUDENT.

When prompted, enter your full school email address

(firstname.lastname@heretaunga.school.nz)

and the password for your school email address.

If you are asked if you want to continue connecting, click 'Connect'.

You are now connected to HC-STUDENT
