School Socials

Heretaunga College has official Facebook and Instagram pages, alongside dedicated Heretaunga College Sport accounts, designed to engage students and whānau while improving communication. These platforms promote student work, celebrate achievements, and keep our community informed about school life and events. The Instagram page, launched in early 2021, is run by Renee and overseen by SLT, ensuring content is relevant, respectful, and uplifting. From sports highlights to creative projects, these pages offer a vibrant window into our kura, helping students feel connected and proud. It’s a modern way to share our story and strengthen the bond between school and home.
