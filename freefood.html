Breafast Club and other Food

Heretaunga College’s Breakfast Club runs every day at the Kickstart Pavilion, providing a warm and welcoming start to the morning for all students. It’s a place where you can grab a bite to eat, catch up with friends, and set yourself up for a great day of learning. The Breakfast Club offers free food such as toast, Milo, and Weet-Bix, ensuring everyone has the energy they need to focus in class. Whether you’re running late, forgot to eat at home, or just want to enjoy a relaxed morning atmosphere, the club is open to everyone and is a great way to begin the day feeling nourished and ready to learn.

The school also offers free food on certain days throughout the year, such as Multicultural Day, when food from various cuisines from around the world is available to all students who want to try. Additionally, if you take a Foods or Hospitality class, you will be able to make and eat various foods every week.
