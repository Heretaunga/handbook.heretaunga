Free Sanitary Products



Heretaunga College is committed to supporting the wellbeing and dignity of all students by ensuring that free sanitary products are available for anyone who needs them. We understand that access to pads and tampons is essential for students who menstruate, and we want to make sure that no one has to miss out on learning or school activities because of their period.



Sanitary products are available in an easily accessible location near the SLT offices, so students can collect what they need without embarrassment or having to ask. You are also welcome to take some products for your family members, ensuring support extends beyond the school. This initiative helps promote equality, comfort, and confidence, ensuring that everyone can focus on their learning and fully participate in school life.
