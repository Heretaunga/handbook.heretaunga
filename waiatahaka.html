English Translation

Students stand up, stand with the strength of a high chief
This generation of Heretaunga College is ready to shake the foundations that lay below us.

Descend
From the tip of the mountain Remutaka
To the canoe Tokomaru
To the river Te Awa Kairangi that will never fade
To the marae Orongomai

Our sheltering havens Kahukura and Rongomai

We pay acknowledgments to the people of the land, Te Āti Awa
We acknowledge the people from Heretaunga

Stand up, be ready, be alert
For the children, the grandchildren, and our family
Everybody stand with inspiration, passion, and excitement, stand in solidarity forever and always

Here we make our way to the callings of our school
Be awake, be alert
