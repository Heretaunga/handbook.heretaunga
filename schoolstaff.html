School Staff

Leadership Team:
The Principal and leadership team ensure the school runs smoothly, support students and teachers, and guide the school towards success. The leadership team includes:


Board of Trustees (BOT):
The Board of Trustees helps run the school from behind the scenes. They make big decisions about money, rules, and how the school should grow. They work with the principal to make sure students have the best learning environment.

Principal:
The principal is the boss of the school. They make sure everything runs smoothly, help teachers do their best, and make sure students are safe and learning well. They also talk to families and the community to help the school improve.

Assistant Principals / Deputy Principals:
These leaders help the principal run the school. They help with student behavior, support teachers, and make sure school rules are followed. They’re also there to support students if they need help or advice.

Deans:
Deans look after student behavior, attendance, and well-being for a specific Year Group. They check in when students need help and work with families and teachers to support students. If you're having problems, a dean is someone you can talk to



Classroom and Teaching Staff:
The Principal and leadership team ensure the school runs smoothly, support students and teachers, and guide the school towards success. The leadership team includes:


Heads of Departments (HODs):
HODs are in charge of subjects like maths, English, or science. They help teachers plan lessons and make sure students are learning well. They also make sure the subject is fun and interesting for everyone.

Teachers:
Teachers help you learn new things and do your best at school. They plan lessons, explain ideas, and help when you’re stuck. Teachers care about your learning and want you to succeed.

Kaiarahi / PRIDE Teachers:
These teachers check in with you every day or week to see how you’re doing. They’re in charge of a specific class and all the students in that class. They help with goal setting, school values, and make sure you feel supported. They’re someone you can trust and talk to if you need help.



Support Staff:
The Principal and leadership team ensure the school runs smoothly, support students and teachers, and guide the school towards success. The leadership team includes:


Teacher Aides:
Teacher aides help students in class. They might work with students who need extra help with reading, writing, or staying focused. They’re kind and patient and want everyone to do well.

Other School Staff (Receptionists, Financial Staff, Caretakers, etc.):
These people help the school run smoothly. The receptionist answers phones and helps visitors. The finance team handles money and school payments. The caretaker fixes things and keeps the school clean and safe. There are many more people in our system who help, even though you may not always see them.
