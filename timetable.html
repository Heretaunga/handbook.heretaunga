Timetable

How to Read a Timetable:


Top Row:
The title shows it’s your school timetable for 2025. The “Term” and “Week” let you know which part of the year it’s for. The class code (like "9WOD") shows your Year 9 class group.

Days of the Week:
Each column is a day – from Monday to Friday. Each day has the same structure: CheckConn, Spell 1 to Spell 5, and two breaks.

Subjects & Codes:
Each subject has a short code. Some examples are:

  • MAT9 – Maths
  • SCI9 – Science
  • ENG9 – English
  • PRIDEM9 – PRIDE Class


Teacher Codes:
Each subject has a teacher code beside it, like CR (Mr Carter), LEO (Mrs Leota), or KRA (Mr Krakovsky). These show who is teaching that class.

Classrooms:
Locations are written using short room codes like MATHS4 (Maths Wing 4), CAL2 (Callaghan 2), or GRA4 (Grace 4). These tell you where each class is held.

Support Staff:
At the bottom, you’ll see your:

  • Tutor – The teacher who looks after your class overall, your PRIDE teacher/Kaiarahi.
  • Dean / Year Level – The person who supports your year group.
  • Management – A senior staff member overseeing school operations.
