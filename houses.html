The House System

At Heretaunga College we run a house system. There are four houses and all students and staff members are allocated one. Each house is appropriately named after significant taonga (treasures) in Upper Hutt.

They are:
Tokomaru (yellow)
Orongomai (red)
Remutaka (green)
Te Awa Kairangi (blue).

Each house has an executive student leader that is in charge of sorting everything house related. They choose their own leaders to help them and they also have a staff member in charge to support them. You must attend all house meetings.

Some house events include:
Be Active Day
Lunchtime sports competitions
HC Day
Other lunchtime events e.g. Fear Factor
Reading challenge (Library)

On all day events, such as Be Active Day and HC Day, students and staff come dressed in their colours.
